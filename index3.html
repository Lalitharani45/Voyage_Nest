End the day with a memorable sunset cruise along the coast.

Visit local markets to experience the vibrant culture and try fresh seafood cuisine.
Highlight: Beach Hopping
Explore different beaches in the area, each with its own charm.

Day 4: Spa and Leisure
Indulge in a relaxing spa treatment or yoga session by the beach.
Highlight: Cultural Evening
Attend a cultural evening featuring traditional music and dance.

Day 5: Departure
Check out from your accommodation and depart from the beach paradise.
Take with you fond memories of your beach vacation!
